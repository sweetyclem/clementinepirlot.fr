Mission Focused Backend developer

Currently studying development in Lyon, France, I have also been admitted to IT school 42 in Paris and will start there in 2017. I believe that technology can be a driving force and can contribute to creating a more equal worl. Equality, and in particular Women’s Studies have been a continuous presence in my education, leading me to computer science.


While studying abroad in Long Beach, California I wrote my dissertation on working women in 1940's Hollywood and upon returning to France I completed a master's degree in the Sociology of Gender and wrote a dissertation on the situation of women in a French computer science school.


The IT sector has always seemed attractive to me and during my experiences working for non profits I was always the tech savvy one. After completing a seven month contract as a project coordinator with the nonprofit Social Builder, helping women move into the digital sector and telling women they can code, the question came naturally, why not me ?


I then started taking every course on Codecademy and love it so much I decided to make it a career. My variousexperiences made me acquire many skills that are my strength and make me a very literate geek. My dedication to code is sustained by a rich life with many interests that allow me to contribute in a meaningful way and to find true personal happiness.
